Welcome to the Leiserson Research Group at the University of Maryland!

We are a research group in the Department of Computer Science and Center for Bioinformatics and Computational Biology at the University of Maryland. Our research is focused on designing algorithms to make advances in public health, particularly related to cancer. Current topics of interest include cancer genomics, cancer immunotherapy, and network biology.
