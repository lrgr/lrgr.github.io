Tracy Chen (Ph.D. student)

Yuexi (Tracy) Chen is currently a 2nd year Ph.D. in computer science (expected 2023). Her main research interests are in two fields:

  1. Modeling mutational signatures in cancer genomes. (advised by Prof. Leiserson)
  2. Developing interactive biological data analysis tools. (advised by Prof. Fushman)

She comes from Sichuan, a southwestern Chinese Province and received her B.S. from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
