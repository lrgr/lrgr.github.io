Welles Robinson (Ph.D. student)

Welles is pursuing his Ph.D. in computer science. He is funded through a cancer research training award (CRTA) from the National Cancer Institute and is co-advised by Eytan Ruppin. His research focuses on modeling DNA repair and damage process, with a focus on carcinogenesis.
